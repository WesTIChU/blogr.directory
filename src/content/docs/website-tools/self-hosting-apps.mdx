Tools that connect directly to running a website — from forums and comments to monitoring and analytics.

---

### Communities & Forums

* <a href="https://www.discourse.org" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><strong>Discourse</strong></a> – Modern forum software used by many large communities.
* <a href="https://flarum.org" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><strong>Flarum</strong></a> – Lightweight and clean forum platform.
* <a href="https://nodebb.org" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><strong>NodeBB</strong></a> – Real-time forum powered by Node.js.

---

### Comments & Interaction

* <a href="https://isso-comments.de" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><strong>Isso</strong></a> – Self-hosted alternative to Disqus.
* <a href="https://utteranc.es" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><strong>Utterances</strong></a> – GitHub-based comment system.
* <a href="https://giscus.app" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><strong>Giscus</strong></a> – Modern comment system powered by GitHub Discussions.

---

### Analytics (Privacy-Friendly)

* <a href="https://plausible.io" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><strong>Plausible</strong></a> – Simple, privacy-focused analytics.
* <a href="https://umami.is" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><strong>Umami</strong></a> – Lightweight alternative to Google Analytics.
* <a href="https://matomo.org" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><strong>Matomo</strong></a> – Full-featured analytics platform.

---

### Monitoring & Uptime

* <a href="https://github.com/louislam/uptime-kuma" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><strong>Uptime Kuma</strong></a> – Monitor your website uptime with a clean dashboard. <Badge text="POPULAR" size="small" />
* <a href="https://healthchecks.io" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><strong>Healthchecks</strong></a> – Monitor cron jobs and background tasks.
* <a href="https://cachethq.io" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><strong>Cachet</strong></a> – Status page system for your services.

---

### Newsletters & Email

* <a href="https://listmonk.app" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><strong>Listmonk</strong></a> – Self-hosted newsletter and mailing list manager.
* <a href="https://www.mailtrain.org" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><strong>Mailtrain</strong></a> – Email marketing platform you host yourself.

---

### Automation & Integrations

* <a href="https://n8n.io" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><strong>n8n</strong></a> – Automate workflows between your apps and services.
* <a href="https://www.activepieces.com" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><strong>Activepieces</strong></a> – Open-source automation platform.

### Why these tools?

These tools help you go beyond just publishing a website — they let you build a real, independent online presence.

From communities to analytics, you control everything instead of relying on third-party platforms.
