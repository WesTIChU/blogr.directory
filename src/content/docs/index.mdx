## Why this exists

The internet used to feel more personal — like wandering through people’s thoughts rather than scrolling through noise.

That feeling is disappearing.

This site is an attempt to bring a small part of it back.

<CardGrid>
	<Card title="Curated, not automated" icon="check">
		Every link is added by hand. If it’s here, it’s because it feels worth your time.
	</Card>
	<Card title="No dead internet sludge" icon="close">
		No AI spam. No empty SEO pages. No mass-produced filler pretending to be useful.
	</Card>
	<Card title="Built for discovery" icon="compass">
		The goal is not to overwhelm you with links — it’s to help you find something genuinely worth reading.
	</Card>
</CardGrid>

<Aside type="caution" title="What you won’t find here">
This is not a giant dump of backlinks, scraped content, or AI-generated junk. If it doesn’t feel human, it doesn’t belong here.
</Aside>
