## Why this exists

The internet used to feel more personal — like wandering through people’s thoughts rather than scrolling through noise.

That feeling is disappearing.

This site is an attempt to bring a small part of it back.

A place to:
- discover voices you’ve never heard before  
- read something written with care  
- and reconnect with the human side of the web
