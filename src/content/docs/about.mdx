This site is a curated collection of **human-written websites, blogs, and corners of the internet worth visiting**.

No AI-generated content farms.  
No recycled SEO nonsense.  
Just real people writing real things.

---

### What this site is

Think of this as a **directory of the human web**.

Everything listed here has been:
- Found manually  
- Checked manually  
- Added because it actually feels worth your time  

You won’t find thousands of links dumped on a page — only sites that have personality, depth, or something interesting to say.

---

### What you’ll find

Across the site, you’ll come across:

- Personal blogs and life writing  
- Travel stories and experiences  
- Tech and developer blogs  
- Creative and niche websites  
- Small communities and forums  
- Hidden gems you wouldn’t normally find  

Each category is organised so you can browse properly — not just scroll endlessly.

---

### Why this exists

The internet is full of:
- AI-written articles  
- Affiliate spam  
- Copy-and-paste content  

This is the opposite of that.

This is about:
- Real voices  
- Independent websites  
- Content made by people, not machines  

---

### How sites get added

There’s no automation here.

Every site is:
- Added manually  
- Placed in a category that fits  
- Given a short description  

If it doesn’t feel human, it doesn’t go on the site.

---

### Tech stack

Built to be fast, simple, and reliable:

- **Astro** – static site framework  
- **Starlight** – documentation-style layout  
- **Markdown** – all content written manually  
- **Deployed via GitHub Pages / Cloudflare**  

No databases. No tracking scripts. No bloat.

---

### Submit a site

If you know a site worth adding, feel free to submit it.

Good fits:
- Personal blogs  
- Unique or niche websites  
- Interesting projects  
- Independent creators  

Not a fit:
- AI-generated content  
- Thin affiliate sites  
- Generic SEO blogs

### Final note

This isn’t about building the biggest list.

It’s about building a **good one**.
