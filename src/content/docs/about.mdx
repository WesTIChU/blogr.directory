## Why this exists

### What’s wrong with the web now

The modern web is full of things that look useful for about three seconds.

AI-written articles with no point of view.  
Affiliate pages pretending to be recommendations.  
Copy-and-paste content written to rank instead of say anything.  
Websites chasing traffic with nothing behind them.

### What this is trying to bring back

This exists because the web is better when you can still stumble across something real.

A weird personal blog.  
A thoughtful essay.  
A niche site made by someone who clearly cares.  
A page that feels like a person, not a strategy.

<Aside type="caution" title="What you won’t find here">
This isn’t a place for AI content, affiliate spam, or content written just to rank.
</Aside>

---

## How sites get added

### The process

There’s no automation here.

Everything is done by hand.

A site gets found, checked, then placed where it actually fits.  
A short description gets written properly, not generated.

### Why it’s slower (and better)

That means things move slower. That’s intentional.

<Aside type="tip" title="The rule">
If it doesn’t feel human, it doesn’t go on the site.
</Aside>
