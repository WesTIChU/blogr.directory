## Why this exists

The modern web is full of things that look useful for about three seconds:

- AI-written articles with no point of view  
- affiliate pages pretending to be recommendations  
- copy-and-paste content written to rank, not to say anything  
- websites with traffic goals but no soul  

This site exists because the web is better when you can still stumble across:

- a weird personal blog  
- a thoughtful essay  
- a niche site made by someone who clearly cares  
- a page that feels like a person, not a strategy  

<Aside type="caution" title="What you won’t find here">
This site is not for AI-generated content, thin affiliate sites, generic SEO blogs, or mass-produced junk with nothing to say.
</Aside>

---

## How sites get added

There’s no automation here.

Every site is:

- found manually  
- checked manually  
- placed in a category that fits  
- given a short description by hand  

That means the list grows slower. That’s deliberate.

<Aside type="tip" title="The rule">
If it doesn’t feel human, it doesn’t go on the site.
</Aside>
