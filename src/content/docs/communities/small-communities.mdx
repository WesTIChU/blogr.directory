A curated collection of smaller communities where people gather around shared interests.

No noise, no endless feeds, just focused discussion and genuine interaction.

### Creative & Craft Communities

* <a href="https://www.ravelry.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><strong>Ravelry</strong></a> – Massive knitting, crochet, and crafting community.
* <a href="https://www.furaffinity.net/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><strong>Fur Affinity</strong></a> – Long-running creative community with a strong niche culture.

### Music & Creative Skills

* <a href="https://www.ultimate-guitar.com/forum/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><strong>Ultimate Guitar</strong></a> – Musicians sharing tabs, techniques, and advice.
* <a href="https://gearspace.com/board/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><strong>Gearspace</strong></a> – Audio production, recording, and music tech discussions.

### Classic Internet Communities

* <a href="https://forums.somethingawful.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><strong>Something Awful</strong></a> – Old-school forum with a distinct internet culture.
* <a href="https://www.gaiaonline.com/forum/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><strong>Gaia Online</strong></a> – Forum-based social community with avatars and a virtual economy.
* <a href="https://www.neogaf.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><strong>NeoGAF</strong></a> – Long-running gaming and culture discussions.

### Learning, Tech & Interests

* <a href="https://forum.freecodecamp.org/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><strong>freeCodeCamp Forum</strong></a> – Programming help and learning community.
* <a href="https://discuss.python.org/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><strong>Python Discussions</strong></a> – Official Python community forum.
* <a href="https://forum.arduino.cc/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><strong>Arduino Forum</strong></a> – Electronics, DIY tech, and maker projects.

### Lifestyle & Niche Interests

* <a href="https://permies.com/forums" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><strong>Permies</strong></a> – Homesteading, sustainability, and off-grid living.
* <a href="https://www.birdforum.net/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><strong>BirdForum</strong></a> – Birdwatching and wildlife discussion community.

## Why niche communities matter

The best parts of the internet aren’t always the biggest platforms, they’re the smaller spaces where people genuinely care.

These communities are built around shared interests, not algorithms.

And that’s what keeps them alive.
